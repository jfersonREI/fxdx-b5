FEMA Data Exchange (FEMADex)

FEMADex provides more rapid, responsive, and available analytics through tools and features that allow robust collaboration and interoperability with internal and external systems. The system is designed to support FEMA through disaster preparation, disaster response, and disaster recovery operations, providing staff and leadership a faster path to data-driven decisions.
